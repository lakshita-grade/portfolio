Cloud Z

Ground Floor Plan

The ground floor has cabins of different department like- Small meeting room, Merchandising cabin, a Finance cabin, Marketing company. The merchandising head cabin is open to the corridor to showcase the products. The common workstation area is open to the reception area. The reception desk is under the staircase which makes it a part of the staircase. The staircase is used as an informal area to sit around with extended tread in the double height.

First Floor Plan

The staircase leading to the entrance of the conference room makes it approachable to clients. The conference room has a logo of the company which makes it vibrant. The co-producer's office is open to make it easy to interact with the clients and employees. The branch manager's office is overlooking the outside glass facade to make it more interactive with the outside world. The pantry and the ancillary are very informal with casual seating and games.

Isometric Plan depicting the Exposed Ceiling

The exposed ceiling is done to give a huge impact in the space with heavy ceiling impact. The exposed ceiling gives an industrial nature to the office. It also makes the servicing of the HVAC or electrical easy. Exposed conduits run throughout the space with multiple conduits running together. These are further branching in the space. The lighting is mostly used as a pendant of profile light to give the space lighting at different levels.

Storage Unit

Storage in the conference room is designed in a way that it is used to store the VR screen and Ar glasses but also when closed can be used as a display area or an art piece. This makes the space looks very vibrant and usable. Storage has the opening shutter in upwards and downwards directions. The uneven tessellations make it look more interactive. The closing system looks like an interlocking system.

Reception Desk

The table is designed in such a way that it becomes part of the angular dynamic staircase. The reception desk was placed under the staircase with one end continuing from the side riser making it like a small seating and the other end attached to the column. The reception desk is bent in such a form that one place is used to keep the 3d magazines and the tablets for the visitors. The flat surface is used to keep the VR glasses for the virtual experience of the space.

Concept

The aluminium box section, maple wood, and glass are used to give an industrial feel to the space and make it an interactive and transparent space. The colours show the industrial and bold nature of the space which goes as an impact full-colour scheme with the materials.
